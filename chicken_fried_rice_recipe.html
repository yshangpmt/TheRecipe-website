Chicken Fried Rice Recipe

Chicken fried rice is a classic Asian dish that combines tender chicken pieces with savory vegetables, fluffy rice, and aromatic seasonings. It's a flavorful and satisfying meal that can be enjoyed any time of the day.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon oil, peanut, vegetable or canola
  • 2 eggs, lightly whisked
  • 150g chicken breast, finely sliced into small pieces
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 120g bacon , excess fat trimmed, chopped
  • 1 small carrot, peeled and diced 0.75cm
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn (or can or fresh)
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cups (packed) cooked white rice, day old
  • 3/4 cup green onions , finely sliced
  • 2.5 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine or Mirin
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 3 tablespoon light soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper

Instructions:

  1. Mix Sauce in a bowl. Pour 2 teaspoon over chicken, toss to coat, then set aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a wok or skillet over medium heat. Add egg and scramble until just cooked (still a touch wet) then remove.
  3. Turn heat up to high, add remaining 1/2 tablespoon oil and bacon into same skillet. Cook for 1 minute or so until fat starts to melt, then add onion and garlic.
  4. Cook until bacon is light golden - about 1 1/2 minutes.
  5. Add vegetables (still frozen is fine). Cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add chicken and cook until it changes from pink to white - about 1 1/2 minutes.
  7. Add rice and Sauce ingredients. Cook for 1 1/2 minutes, stirring consantly.
  8. Add egg and green onion, toss through to disperse then remove from heat.
  9. Serve immediately!
